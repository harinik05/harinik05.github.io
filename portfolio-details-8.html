Project Purpose

The product, Harini’s Gifts Kart, is a GUI based application that assists small businesses to manage their daily sales, stock inventories, and generate analytic reports. Shopping Cart: Products can be purchased and stocks are updated in the database Add Products: Products can be added and updated in the products database Inventory Reports: The reports of products that are low on stock can be emailed to the stakeholders as a pdf file Donation: In the shopping cart, there is an option to donate for social good or store external purchases Invoices: The final amount is calculated for all of the products in the checkout Sales Reports: Final amounts are calculated in daily, monthly, and yearly timings Email Configurations: The email can be configured according to the admin’s preferences. Once set, it doesn’t need to be changed
