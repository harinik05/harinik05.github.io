About

My name is Harini Karthik and I am currently a computer engineering student at UWaterloo. My passion for science was sparked by volunteering at parks. I love to bike on trails and go for hikes during my free time. In science fair projects from previous years, I was inspired by the natural phenomenon of helicopter seeds for designing a wind turbine. Outside the classroom, I founded a STEM club at my school with my friend to promote science for everyone through weekly challenges. I was also an executive member of the environmental club and co-founded Oakville Green Scene to provide eco-friendly volunteer opportunities for high school students in my community. During summer, I volunteer for Mary’s Meals Canada to spread awareness of youth education and world hunger. I am currently looking for a summer co-op dealing with web development and serverless technology.
